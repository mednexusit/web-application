Key Highlights

  • Renowned Speakers:

    Learn from leading experts, researchers & practitioners from India & around the world.

  • Interactive Sessions:

    Participate in workshops, panel discussions, & poster presentations covering diverse topics.

  • Cutting-edge Research:

    Showcase your own research or discover groundbreaking findings in various forensic medicine subspecialties.

  • Networking Opportunities:

    Connect with colleagues, peers, & potential collaborators from across the globe.

  • Cultural Immersion:

    Immerse yourself in the rich culture & heritage of Chennai on a specially curated tour.

Conference Tracks

  • • Forensic Medicine and Toxicology
  • • Clinical Forensic Medicine
  • • Forensic Psychiatry and Psychology
  • • Forensic Odontology & Anthropology
  • • DNA Analysis & Forensic Genetics
  • • Forensic Serology & Immunology
  • • Digital Forensics & Cybercrime
  • • Medico-Legal & Law
  • • Ethical Issues & Professional Development

Why Attend?

  • • Sharpen Your skills & knowledge with the latest advancement in Forensic Medicine.
