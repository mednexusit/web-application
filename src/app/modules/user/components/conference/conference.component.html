Cancellation and Refund Policy for Users
User-Initiated Cancellations

  1. Cancellation Timeframes and Refunds:

    Once registered no refunds .
    User will receive 100% refund if conference is cancelled .
    - No-shows: Users who do not attend the event are not eligible for any refund.

  2. Rescheduling by the Organizer:

    If an event is rescheduled, users will have the option to:
    - Attend the event on the new date, or
    - Request a full refund. Users must inform MednexusNS of their choice within [Number of Days, e.g., 7 days] from the date of notification of the rescheduling.

  3. Refund Processing for Users:

    - Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days from the date the cancellation is confirmed.
    - All refunds will be credited to the original payment method used during registration.

  4. Force Majeure:

    In cases where events are canceled due to unforeseen circumstances beyond the control of the organizer (e.g., natural disasters, pandemics), refunds will be handled according to the specific terms provided by the organizer for such events. Users will be notified of these terms promptly.

  5. Exceptions:

    Certain events may have special cancellation and refund policies, which will be clearly outlined in the event details. Users are encouraged to review these details before registering.

  6. Limitation of Liability:

    MednexusNS shall not be held liable for any damages, costs, or losses incurred due to event cancellations, postponements, or changes made by vendors or due to unforeseen circumstances.

  7. User Responsibilities:

    Users are responsible for providing accurate contact and payment information and for responding promptly to communications from MednexusNS regarding cancellations, rescheduling, and refunds.

  8. User Dispute Resolution Process:

    Any disputes regarding cancellations or refunds should first be directed to MednexusNS customer support at [support@mednexusns.com]. If a resolution cannot be reached through customer support, users agree to resolve disputes through mediation or arbitration before pursuing legal action.

  9. Notice and Communication Protocol:

    All notices regarding cancellations, rescheduling, or refunds must be provided in writing via the registered email address or through the MednexusNS platform notification system.

  10. Compliance with Local Laws:

    This policy is subject to and will be interpreted in accordance with applicable local, state, and national laws regarding consumer rights and protections.

Cancellation and Refund Policy for Vendors
Vendor-Initiated Cancellations and Rescheduling

  1. Cancellation Requirements:

    - Vendors must provide at least 30 days' notice to MednexusNS and registered users if an event needs to be canceled or rescheduled.
    - If a vendor cancels or reschedules an event with less than 30 days' notice, they may not be required to cover any additional costs incurred by users, such as travel and accommodation expenses.

  2. Vendor Refund Obligations:

    - If an event is canceled by the vendor, the vendor agrees to offer a full refund to all registered users, including any processing fees, facilitated through MednexusNS.
    - In cases of rescheduling, if users opt for a refund instead of attending on the new date, the vendor is responsible for covering the full refund amount.

  3. Refund Processing for Vendors:

    - MednexusNS will handle the refund process on behalf of the vendor, with the vendor reimbursing MednexusNS for all refund amounts paid to users.
    - Vendors are required to cooperate fully with MednexusNS to ensure timely refunds to users.

  4. Penalties for Late Cancellations:

    Vendors who cancel or reschedule events with less than the required notice period may face penalties, including but not limited to covering user-incurred costs and potential suspension from the platform for repeated violations.

  5. Vendor Rescheduling Terms:

    - If an event is rescheduled, vendors must clearly communicate the new date and any changes to the event details to MednexusNS and registered users as soon as possible.
    - Vendors are responsible for addressing any user concerns or questions about the rescheduled event.

  6. Force Majeure for Vendors:

    In the event of cancellation due to force majeure, vendors must follow the agreed-upon terms with MednexusNS and communicate any specific refund policies to users promptly.

  7. Limitation of Liability for Vendors:

    MednexusNS shall not be liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising out of event cancellations or rescheduling initiated by vendors.

  8. Vendor Responsibilities:

    Vendors are responsible for providing accurate event details, maintaining open communication with users, and adhering to MednexusNS policies to avoid disputes and penalties.

  9. Vendor Dispute Resolution Process:

  10. Compliance with Local Laws:

    Vendors must ensure that all events listed and managed on MednexusNS comply with applicable local, state, and national laws.

  11. Insurance Requirements:

    Vendors shall maintain adequate insurance coverage to protect against any liabilities that may arise from the events.

  12. Contact for Vendor Refunds:

    Vendors with questions or concerns regarding cancellations, refunds, or rescheduling should contact their designated MednexusNS account manager or email [vendor support@mednexusns.com].
